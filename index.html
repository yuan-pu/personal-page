About

Hi! I'm Yuan Pu (浦媛).

Recently graduated from Brown University with a B.S. in computational biology (departmental honors and manga cum laude), I have a keen interest in applying computational tools to solve biological questions. In addition to completing interdisciplinary coursework, I spend much time doing research projects on biological data using statistical analysis and deep learning.

For my leisure time, I make art, read fiction, and play Mahjong.

Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you soon :)
